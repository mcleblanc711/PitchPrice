About PitchPrice

PitchPrice tracks hotel room rates in Toronto and Vancouver for the FIFA World Cup 2026 game dates. Montreal is included as a control city to measure event-driven price increases. Data is collected twice weekly from Booking.com and displayed here to help travelers understand pricing trends and find the best deals.
